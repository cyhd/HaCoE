22 /**********************************************************************************************************
23 HRExperiment class
24 
25 Created: May 2013
26 Author: Devin Luu, student ict, CSIRO
27 
28 This class contains all parameters for a single Haptic Replication Experiment.
29 It defines:
30 force magnitude
31 force direction ( x or z )
32 force direction ( + or - )
33 force sequence (sequential or simultaneous)
34 force presentation side (dominant or non-dominant side)
35 
36 For a full haptic replication experiment, one object per trial will be created.
37 They can be put in a vector and then randomized.
38 *************************************************************************************************************/
